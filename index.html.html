How It Works
1. Pick a preset or build from scratch.
Presets fill everything in for you. You can tweak after.

2. Select chips to build your sound.
Click Genre, Mood, Production, Structure, and Avoid chips. Add your own custom ones too.

3. Hit Generate Prompt.
Three outputs appear: Title, Song Details, and Final Prompt. Copy what your AI tool needs.

4. Check the Suggestions panel.
It tells you what to fix or improve before you paste into your AI music tool.

5. Save your setup.
Hit Save Setup to store your current build. Load Setup brings it back this session.
Tip: Suno vs General
Suno — Keep it short and tight. Suno reads genre tags better than long sentences.

MusicGPT / Udio — More detail works well. Give it clear vocal and mood framing.

Donna — Emotional arc and sonic descriptors. Give it a dramatic build.

General — Works across all tools. Balanced detail and direction.
